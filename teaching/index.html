    Arranging supervisions

    If you would like a supervision in one of the courses listed above, please email me at eww23@cam.ac.uk. Include contact details (CRSids) for all students in the supervision group.

    It's best to arrange supervisions starting as early as possible in the term.

    Handing in work

    My preferred method of receiving work is email. Please email me at eww23@cam.ac.uk with your work attached as a .pdf document.

    If this is not possible for some reason, then please hand it in at Student Administration at the Computer Lab – place it in the letterbox.

    I also accept work handed in at Gonville & Caius College porter's lodge, but please notify me if you do this, as I do not often pass through there.

    For practical work, see specific instructions for each course.

    • Part IB Computer graphics & image processing – Easel
    • Part II Computer vision – OpenCV

    Deadlines

    Work should be handed in by 16:00 the day before the supervision.

    Incomplete or missing work

    If you are struggling to complete the work, that's OK. But please notify me as early as possible explaining what you are stuck on so I can prepare a useful explaination or example for the supervision.

    Incomplete supervision work is fine, but failing to hand anything in is not.
