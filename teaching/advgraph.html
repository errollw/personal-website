Practical exercises

Write vertex and fragment shaders for a procedural fireball object, or similar. Consider:

  • Using Perlin or simplex noise functions.
  • Create an undulating surface with a displacement map.
  • Use a colour-map to produce fire-like colours.
  • Add post-processing effects, e.g. bloom.
  • Add particles, e.g. embers or smoke.
