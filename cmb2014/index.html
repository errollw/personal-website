Gonville & Caius May Ball

Tuesday 17 June 2014

This year Caius May Ball pays homage to Stephen Hawking, one of our most distinguished alumni. Hawking wrote this world-changing publication during his time at Caius, and his writings have inspired us to explore the boundaries of time and space. Follow us through the wormhole on Tuesday 17th June…

Food & Drink

Prepare yourselves for a feast for the eyes and the tongue – hot and mild, sweet and savoury, naughty and nice. With no queues to be seen on the night, ball-goers will have the opportunity to try dishes ranging from the locally sourced to the truly exotic and to imbibe a dizzying range of beverages tailored to suit all tastes.

Add one part of the familiar, two parts of something novel and shake together in Caius college. Serve over crushed spectacle and add pomp to taste.

Music

Caius has always delivered some of the finest musical entertainment May Week has to offer. With previous crowd-pleasing bands and DJs ranging from The Feeling to Hot Chip, and Cambridge favourites Truly Medly Deeply and The Brass Funkeys, we promise there'll be something for everyone all night long.

Entertainment

Caius May Ball provides a truly unique blend of entertainment, merging ethereal performers and acts alongside some more ‘hands-on’ fun – it promises to be exceptional.

We are now auditioning for all types of acts. Please apply online here, or contact us for more information.
