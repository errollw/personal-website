Caius May Ball 2014 worker roles

Area Supervisor

Area Supervisors will be responsible for the smooth running of an entire court during the ball.

Area Supervisors generally have significant previous experience in both a similar role at other balls and in other events management, such as on May Ball committees, and they can expect to receive signnificant responsibility as well as substantial financial remuneration - up to £120-£150 for supervisors who work the whole night, depending on experience.

Team Leader

Team Leaders will be in charge of one area of the ball, such as a bar or food stand, and are responsible for making sure that area is working.

In charge of a team of 3-5 people, they must be responsible and adaptable, able to both keep track of details of inventory as well as possessing the people skills to motivate their team while also serving guests themselves. Team Leaders will have experience in the role they are applying for before ie a Bar Team Leader will have previous bar experience, ideally at a May Ball. Team Leaders will receive extra training and can expect very competitive wages, around £8-10 per hour depending on experience.

Team Member

Team Members are the core of our workforce and will be responsible for making the ball run smoothly and making sure guests have the best party of their year!

Team Members who work the first shift will attend the second half of the ball as a guest; Team Members who work the second shift or in the morning will be paid.
